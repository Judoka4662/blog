Data storage for dollar store prices!

Google One is now open to all

Posted by Peter Sifuentez on August 15, 2018

Are you tired of storing data on your usb then washing your clothes with them in your pockets, is this a problem? Fear not Google is here!

The tech titan accounced a new subscription for it's Google drive, new users in the USA can sign up for a subscription on Google one.

Plans start 100 GB for $1.99, 200 GB for $2.99, and 2 TB for $9.99 a month. Need more data? No problem! The tech titan will sell you 10 TB - 30 TB bewtten $99.99 and $299.99 a month. If your building a new AI, I'm sure you'll be onboard with these prices.

In Data We Trust.

We can all agree that consumers will benefit with these storage prices that are comparable to shopping at the local dollar store. Watch out Jeff bezos! Besides the storage prices, Google also has added more additional perks.

Such as 24/7 tech support, getting great deals on on hotels that you have found using Google maps, giving credits on Google play, and the titan has said it will add more offers on Google store and Google express.

Unfortunately Google one is only being offered in the USA, however, Google has said it has plans to roll out to other countries soon.
